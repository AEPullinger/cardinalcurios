Bar Stools

Start Date: 5/7/2021
Projected End Date: 6/7/2021

These two stools will be sanded down to remove all of the wear and tear from years of use. Once the surface is completely cleaned, we will paint the wood a nice bright white to match with the chosen fabric (shown in picture). The leather on top is going to be removed, the padding inside the seats is going to be replaced, and this new fabric will be used to upholster these two stools.

Let us know if you are interested in buying this project once it is finished and we will make sure to set it aside for you!
