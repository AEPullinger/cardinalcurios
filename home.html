About Us

After my mother moved out of her home, I had to take on the task of selling many of the items she had collected over all her decades of life. My daughter agreed to help me and so we started to catalogue all of the items and prepare them for sale. In the process, we realized we had some extra furniture that needed to be sold off as well, not to mention all the furniture projects that my daughter takes on for fun! With all of these items coming together, along with the restrictions of Covid-19, Cardinal Curios was born.

We are currently based in Gurnee, Illinois. If you are out of state and would like one of our pieces, please feel free to contact us so we can figure something out as far as delivery! Please note that you will be in charge of paying the fees associated with this as we are not a delivery company.

If you have any other questions about our pieces, current or upcoming, let us know!
